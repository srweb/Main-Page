2014 Golf Tournament Promo page  (before hand-leading up to event)



<p><strong><span style="font-size: x-large;">19th Annual Golf Tournament</span></strong></p>
<p><img src="images/layout/pandy-golf-tee.png" border="0" alt="Golf Tee" width="110" height="208" style="float: left;" />The SRHS Foundation will hold its 19th Annual Golf Tournament on Friday, September 12, 2014 at the Oakmont West Course.&nbsp; The proceeds from this tournament benefit SRHS student academic and sports programs.</p>
<p>All graduates, friends and supporters of SRHS are invited to play in this well attended and fun tournament.&nbsp; The Flights are Men&rsquo;s, Coed and Women&rsquo;s.&nbsp; We continue to encourage more women&rsquo;s teams to participate and a special rate is available for our Super Seniors (75 years and over).&nbsp; Following golf is our dinner and action packed silent and live auction.&nbsp; Non golfer SRHS supporters and non golfing spouses are encouraged to join us for these post golf activities that includes a no-host cocktail party at 5 PM.</p>
<p>If you, your class or your company would like to support us as a Tee Sponsor, the cost is only $150 and you will have a large sign with your requested name placed on one of the holes and recognition listing in the tournament handouts.&nbsp; For questions about becoming a sponsor or about the tournament in general, please call the Pandy line at <span style="text-decoration: underline;">707-571-7747</span> or email <span style="text-decoration: underline;">golf@srhsf.org</span>.&nbsp; A tournament registration form is available on our website.</p>
