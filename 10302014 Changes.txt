<h1>One Thousand Panthers on Parade!</h1>
<p>The SRHS Foundation is proud to announce its new <strong>One Thousand Pathers</strong> fundraising campaign!&nbsp; The challenge for this campaign will be to raise $1,000,000 in <strong>permanent endowment&nbsp;funds</strong> that will benefit students and programs at SRHS for years to come!</p>
<p>The One Thousand Pathers campaign, under the leadership of Ann Porrino, Class of 1961, has developed several ways to participate and all Foundation members and friends of SRHS and the Foundation are invited&nbsp;to become a part of this special project.&nbsp;</p>
<div class="floatRight"><img src="images/layout/paws.png" border="0" alt="" /></div>
<p>The goal is for <strong>1,000 donors to each contribute $1,000 or more</strong> in support of the endowment goal.&nbsp; The earnings from this permanent fund will be used by the Foundation19s Board of Directors&nbsp;to approve funding requests made by students, faculty or administrators.</p>
